Hotlines

Professionally-staffed local & national hotlines which can assist you during a crisis situation.


We hope the resources below will allow you to get the help you need. Many of the services below operate 24/7 and toll-free, and all can be contacted anonymously and confidentially, puttng you in touch with professional counsellors who can help you navigate through your particular situation. Some services may not be available due to COVID-19.

United States

  • Suicide: Suicide Prevention Lifeline (24/7): 1-800-273-8255
  • Child Abuse (Call or Text): Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline (24/7): 1-800-422-4453
  • Child Abuse (Live Chatroom): Childhelp Child Abuse Live Chatroom: https://childhelp.org/childhelp-hotline/
  • Sexual Assault: National Sexual Assault Hotline (24/7): 1-800-656-4673
  • Emergency services: 911

Canada

  • Suicide: Canada Suicide Prevention Service (24/7): 1-833-456-4566
  • Child Abuse (Call or Text): Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline (24/7): 1-800-422-4453
  • Child Abuse (Live Chatroom): Childhelp Child Abuse Live Chatroom: https://childhelp.org/childhelp-hotline/
  • Sexual Assault: Varies by province. For a full list of resources in Canada, visit My Health Alberta.
    • Alberta: Alberta's One Line for Sexual Violence (Call or Text) (9am to 9pm MT): 1-800-403-8000
    • British Columbia and the Yukon: VictimLinkBC (24/7): 1-800-563-0808 or
    • Ontario: Provincial hotlines available at the Ministry of Children, Community and Social Services, or contact Ontario 211.
    • Quebec: Provincial Helpline for Victims of Sexual Assault: 1-888-933-9007
  • Youth Helpline (Call): Kids Help Phone (24/7): 1-800-688-6868
  • Youth Helpline (Text): Kids Help Phone (24/7): 686868
  • Emergency services: 911
